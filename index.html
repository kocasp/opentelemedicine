OpenSource telemedical tool for patients and medical volunteers.

OpenTelemedicine is a modern service designed to streamline access to medical professionals in times of increased demand, using simple but efficient solutions:
  • - Volunteer telemedicine platform for phisicians
  • - Searchable volunteer doctors database
  • - Platform for patient
    • DOCTOR SIGNUP PATIENT SIGNUP

    Key features

    The Time of Crisis

    Telemedicine Platform

    Simple way help people as a volunteer phisician. We understand simplicity is not only as a design principle, but also as a tool to reduce time required to train users.

    Patient Account

    Our system is designed to increase availability and reduce the strain of skilled medical professionals. Post your health issue and wait for doctor to contact you via VOIP or mobile.

    Volunteer Database

    An integral part of the system available for medical facilities. Search for phisicians that declared their availability in the local area.

    Patients and Healthcare Administrators

    Unified Solution

    We understand that, depending on the point of view, a physician may be a healthcare provider or a healthcare worker - the expectations of patients and healthcare administrators differ.

    Professional Advice

    Our aim is to verify our associate physicians (we have experience with automatic verification against Polish Medical Board database) so that the patients may be certain of the quality of advice.

    Simplified Recruitment

    Healthcare administrators will be able to reach willing physicians/volunteers with job offers, reducing time required to recruit staff.

    Physicians

    Safety Net

    We understand that less experienced physicians/volunteers require specialist consult and oversight, we implement tools to allow them to safely help patients according to their competencies.

    Strain Reduction

    Our aim is to reduce the amount of trivial cases reaching experienced specialists, allowing them to fully utilize their valuable skills.

    Flexible Workload

    Healthcare administrators will be able to reach willing physicians/volunteers with job offers, reducing time required to recruit staff.

    Created with support of medical specialists

    Hosted version of the platform is constantly tested and validated in medical facilities.

    Easy health issue posting with intuitive patient interface

    Patient can post health issue or prescription request. Despite volunteer nature of the platform patient can be sure

    • Patient is able to see his current problem and phisician response.
    • Patient profile can be easily accessed via mobile
    • Patient will receive doctors response or prescription via email and sms

    Handle pre-assigned cases with phisician interface

    Each case can be pre-prioritized by any medical volunteer that is able to assign a case to a specialist. Thanks to that, specialists are able to efficiently advice patients assigned to their specialization. The goal is to enable specialists to help remotely in no-time.

    • Case prioritization in the context of specialization
    • Internal comments for patient
    • VOIP service allowing doctor to call patient mobile from inside of the platform.

    Registered phisicians database available as easily exportable Heroku dataclip.

    Phisicians database live database access can be granted per medical facility request.

    • XML, Excel export
    • Real-time update
    • Customizable and location-based
